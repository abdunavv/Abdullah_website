Abdullah Naveed

Hello! I'm Abdullah Naveed, an Electrical Engineering student with a passion for renewable energy and cybersecurity. Growing up in Karachi, Pakistan, where blackouts were a norm, and later moving to Saudi Arabia, where electricity was abundant, sparked my determination to bridge the energy gap in underdeveloped countries. At Florida International University, I'm honing my skills to address these global challenges. My journey is driven by a mission to ensure sustainable, secure energy solutions for all. This website showcases my projects, achievements, and aspirations. Explore my work and connect with me to learn more about how we can light up the world together. My journey has taken me from the blackout-ridden streets of Karachi to the energy-rich environments of Saudi Arabia and the innovative classrooms of Florida International University. I am committed to utilizing renewable energy and cybersecurity to create sustainable solutions for underdeveloped countries.
